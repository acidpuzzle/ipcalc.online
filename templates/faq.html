IP Calculator supports IPv4 and IPv6. Just copy the string with the address and mask from the network device and paste it into the field.

Supported formats:

  • 127.0.0.1
  • 192.168.0.1/24
  • 172.16.44.1 21
  • 192.168.0.1/255.255.255.252
  • 10.0.0.0 / 12
  • 169.254.0.2 16
  • 192.168.24.0 0.0.1.255
  • 192.168.255.1/24 /25
  • fe80::e1b:4dc8:c839:a10%20
  • fe80::e1b:4dc8:c839:a10/20
  • fe80::e1b:4dc8:c839:a10/64 /66
  • fffe::e1b:4dc8:c839:a10

API:

  • https://ipcalc.online/api/192.168.0.1_24
  • https://ipcalc.online/api/192.168.0.1_24_25
